About Us

Welcome to Citizens Voice! This platform allows citizens to raise their issues and problems directly. Our mission is to connect people and authorities by providing a transparent system for problem reporting.

We value your feedback and are committed to resolving the issues faced by the people of Andhra Pradesh. Please use this portal to lodge your grievances or provide suggestions.
