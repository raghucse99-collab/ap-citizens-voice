How can I submit my problem?

You can submit your problem using the form on the Home page.

Do I need to give my personal details?

Name and phone number are optional. You may submit anonymously.

What happens after submission?

Your grievance will be recorded and forwarded to the concerned authority.

Is this an official government website?

No, this is a citizen support initiative to highlight local issues.
